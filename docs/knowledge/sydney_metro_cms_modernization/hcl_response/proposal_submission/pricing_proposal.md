5. **Content Types**: Up to 50 distinct content types and templates

7. **Customization**: Standard Contentful configuration; limited custom development

The fixed price includes:

## 7. Exclusions

The following items are explicitly excluded from this pricing proposal:

### 7.1 Out of Scope Items

1. **Ongoing Operations**: Post-implementation managed services (available as optional service)
2. **Hardware**: Client-side hardware, workstations, or devices
3. **Third-Party Licenses**: Software licenses not specified in Section 3.2
4. **Content Creation**: Creation of new content (migration of existing content is included)
5. **Design Services**: Graphic design, UX/UI design beyond CMS configuration
6. **Custom Development**: Bespoke application development outside core CMS functionality
7. **Data Cleansing**: Extensive data cleansing or content remediation activities
8. **Legacy System Decommissioning**: Retirement or decommissioning of existing systems
9. **Change Management**: Organizational change management programs
10. **Additional Integrations**: System integrations beyond the specified 8 integration points

### 7.2 Change Requests

Changes to the agreed scope will be managed through a formal change request process:

- Change requests will be assessed for impact on timeline, cost, and resources
- Approved changes will be priced using rates specified in Section 5.4
- Material changes may require amendment to the payment schedule
- Change request turnaround: Impact assessment within 5 business days

### 7.3 Assumptions Variance

If actual conditions vary materially from assumptions in Section 6.1, HCL reserves the right to:

- Initiate a change request to adjust scope, timeline, or pricing
- Propose alternative approaches to accommodate variances
- Negotiate contract amendments for substantial deviations

Examples of material variances:
- Content volume exceeding 10,000 items by more than 20%
- Additional integration requirements beyond 8 systems
- Significant delays due to client dependencies exceeding 30 cumulative days
- Material changes to technical architecture or platform selection
