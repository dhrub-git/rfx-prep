ThinkRAM maintains a lean, highly skilled team of 20 professionals who deliver technology transformation services across government, financial services, telecommunications, and energy sectors. Our organizational structure emphasizes technical excellence, client delivery, and continuous learning.

### Company Demographics

**Total Headcount:** 20 full-time employees

**Headquarters:** Sydney, Australia

**Employee Diversity:**
- Gender: 45% female, 55% male

- Educational Background: 90% hold bachelor's degree or higher; 45% hold master's degree

**Geographic Distribution:**

ThinkRAM invests significantly in employee development, training, and certification to maintain technical excellence and ensure staff remain current with emerging technologies and industry best practices.

### Training Program Overview

**Annual Training Budget:** $80,000 AUD ($4,000 per employee average)
**Training Hours per Employee:** 52 hours/year average (1 hour/week)
**Certification Budget:** Included in training budget (100% reimbursement)
**Conference Attendance:** 2-3 major conferences annually (AWS re:Invent, Microsoft Ignite, etc.)

### New Hire Onboarding Program

**Program Status:** Yes – Comprehensive 4-week onboarding program
**Program Duration:** 4 weeks (80 hours)
**Completion Rate:** 100%

**Onboarding Curriculum:**

**Week 1: Company Culture & Operations (20 hours)**
- Company history, values, and culture
- Organizational structure and team introductions
- HR policies, benefits, and administrative setup
- Information security training and security policy
- IT systems access and tool setup
- Office tour and facilities orientation
- First-day lunch with CEO and leadership team

**Week 2: Technical Foundations (20 hours)**
- ThinkRAM technical standards and frameworks
- Development environment setup
- Access to client systems and cloud environments
- Security clearance briefing (if applicable)
- Version control and collaboration tools (Git, Jira, Confluence)
- CI/CD pipeline and DevOps practices
- Code review and quality standards

**Week 3: Delivery Methodology & Client Engagement (20 hours)**
- Agile/Scrum delivery methodology
- ThinkRAM project governance framework
- Client engagement and communication standards
- Status reporting and documentation standards
- Risk and issue management
- Change management procedures
- Quality assurance and testing practices

**Week 4: Project Assignment & Shadowing (20 hours)**
