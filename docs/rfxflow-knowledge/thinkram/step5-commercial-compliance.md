## Pricing Structure

### Standard Rate Card

ThinkRAM maintains transparent, competitive pricing aligned with industry standards for technology consulting services in Australia. Our rates reflect the high caliber of our team, certifications, and proven delivery track record.

#### Labor Rates (Effective January 2025)

| Role Level | Hourly Rate (AUD) | Daily Rate (AUD) | Annual FTE (AUD) |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Executive / Principal Consultant** | $250-$300 | $2,000-$2,400 | $520,000-$624,000 |
| **Lead / Senior Consultant** | $200-$250 | $1,600-$2,000 | $416,000-$520,000 |

### Financial Terms

#### Overhead Rate
**Overhead Rate:** 65%

ThinkRAM's overhead rate of 65% covers:
- Indirect labor (sales, marketing, HR, finance, facilities)
- Fringe benefits (superannuation, health insurance, leave)
- Facilities and infrastructure (office space, IT systems)
- Professional development and certifications
- Insurance (professional liability, cyber, D&O)
- Business development and marketing
- Quality management and compliance
- Tools and software licenses

#### Target Profit Margin
**Target Profit Margin:** 20-25%

ThinkRAM targets a 20-25% profit margin on projects, which is standard for boutique technology consulting firms. This margin supports:
- Business reinvestment and growth
- Innovation and R&D
- Employee bonuses and incentives
- Cash reserves and financial stability
- Shareholder returns

#### Cost-Plus Pricing
For cost-reimbursable contracts:
- Direct labor costs + overhead (65%) + fee (20%)
- Example: $100 direct labor + $65 overhead + $33 fee = $198 billable

## Contracting Terms & Conditions

### Standard Payment Terms

**Payment Terms:** Net 30 days from invoice date
**Alternative Terms Available:**
- Net 45 days (government clients, pre-approved)
- Net 60 days (negotiated for large contracts)
- Net 15 days (with 2% discount)

**Invoicing Schedule:**
- Time & Materials: Monthly in arrears
- Fixed Price: Milestone-based (typical: 20% upfront, 30% at milestones, 20% final)
- Managed Services: Monthly in advance

**Late Payment:**
- Interest charged at 10% per annum on overdue amounts
- Suspension of services after 30 days overdue (with 14 days notice)
- Escalation to collections after 60 days overdue

**Retainage:**
- Acceptable: Up to 10% retention
- Released: Upon final acceptance or 30 days post-completion

### Intellectual Property Rights

**Standard IP Terms:** Client owns work product

ThinkRAM's standard intellectual property approach:

**Client-Owned IP:**
- All custom code, configurations, and deliverables developed specifically for client
- All documentation, designs, and artifacts created for the project
- Transfer of ownership upon final payment
- Source code and documentation provided in escrow or at project completion

**ThinkRAM-Owned IP:**
- Pre-existing ThinkRAM IP, tools, and frameworks
- Generic methodologies, templates, and accelerators
- Reusable code libraries and components developed outside the engagement
- ThinkRAM branding, trademarks, and confidential business information

**Licensed IP:**
- Client granted perpetual, non-exclusive license to use ThinkRAM frameworks and tools deployed in the solution
- No right to redistribute or commercialize ThinkRAM IP
- Support and maintenance included during warranty period

**Third-Party IP:**
- Compliance with all third-party licenses (open source, commercial software)
- Client responsible for licensing of COTS products
- ThinkRAM assists with license procurement and compliance

**Background IP:**
- Each party retains ownership of background IP
- Grant of license to use background IP as necessary for project success

### Limitation of Liability

**Standard Limitation:** Liability capped at contract value (12 months)

ThinkRAM's limitation of liability terms:

**Cap Amount:**
- Standard: Total fees paid in the 12 months prior to claim
- Minimum cap: $1,000,000 AUD
- Maximum cap: $10,000,000 AUD (for very large engagements)

**Exclusions from Cap:**
- Uncapped liability for:
  - Death or personal injury caused by negligence
  - Fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation
  - Willful misconduct or gross negligence
  - Breach of confidentiality obligations
  - Intellectual property infringement
  - Breach of data protection laws

**Consequential Damages:**
- Neither party liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages
- Includes loss of profits, revenue, data, business opportunity
- Exceptions: Fraud, willful misconduct, breach of confidentiality

**Insurance Coverage:**
- Professional Liability: $10M AUD
- Cyber Liability: $15M AUD
- General Liability: $20M AUD
- Insurance certificates provided upon request

### Warranty & Acceptance

**Warranty Period:** 90 days from acceptance

**Warranty Coverage:**
- Software and deliverables free from material defects
- Services performed with reasonable care and skill
- Compliance with specifications and requirements
- Industry-standard workmanship

**Warranty Remedies:**
- Correction of defects at no additional charge
- Re-performance of deficient services
- If correction not feasible: Refund of fees for defective portion

**Warranty Exclusions:**
- Defects caused by client modifications
- Use outside of intended purpose or specifications
- Third-party software or systems (covered by vendor warranty)
- Issues caused by client's infrastructure or environment

**Acceptance Process:**
- User Acceptance Testing (UAT) period: 10-30 business days
- Client issues acceptance certificate or deemed accepted
- Defects identified during UAT corrected at no cost
- Post-acceptance defects covered by warranty

### Termination Provisions

**Termination for Convenience:**
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice
- Client pays for work performed up to termination date
- Client receives all deliverables completed to date
- No termination fees or penalties

**Termination for Cause:**
- Material breach with 14 days cure period
- Insolvency, bankruptcy, or receivership
- Persistent failure to meet performance standards
- Force majeure exceeding 60 days

**Effect of Termination:**
- Final invoice within 14 days
- Return of client property and confidential information
- Transition assistance (up to 30 days)
- Survival of confidentiality, IP, liability provisions

### Subcontracting

**Subcontracting Allowed:** Yes, with client approval

ThinkRAM's subcontracting approach:
- Notification required for all subcontractors
- Client approval required for key positions or >20% of work
- ThinkRAM remains fully responsible for subcontractor performance
- Flow-down of contract terms to subcontractors
- Background checks and security clearances for subcontractors

**Preferred Subcontractors:**
- Strategic partners (AWS, Microsoft, Google)
- Specialty firms (security, data engineering)
- Individual contractors with specific expertise
- Offshore development partners (limited to <10% of work)
